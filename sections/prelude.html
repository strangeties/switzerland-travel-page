Prelude

I explored Switzerland solo over the course of ten days in July 2019.

  • Zürich is a quiet, elegant city.
  • Bern is polished and idyllic. Its Old Town is a medieval district lifted straight out of a fairytale.
  • Mürren is a verdant mountain side repose dotted with chalets. There’s a moment when you realize the mist that sporadically gathers and dissipates is the stuff of clouds.
  • Montreux and Vevey are two small cities with strong French roots that border the northeast coast of Lake Geneva.
  • Geneva is an international city bustling with ambulants from all over the world.

Because photos don't cut through and because traveling on your own can be an introverted, cerebral experience, here are a few vignettes for the memory box (and for your entertainment!).
